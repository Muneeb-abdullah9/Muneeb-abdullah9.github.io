about this item:

Experience the pinnacle of precision and performance with the Rolex Cosmograph Daytona, a legendary timepiece revered by racing enthusiasts and watch connoisseurs alike. Meticulously crafted with the highest standards of Swiss watchmaking, this iconic chronograph blends sporty elegance with unparalleled functionality, making it the ultimate companion for those with a passion for speed and adventure. The Rolex Cosmograph Daytona features a sleek and robust 40mm Oystersteel case, complemented by a distinctive tachymetric bezel crafted from high-tech Cerachrom ceramic, ensuring exceptional durability and resistance to scratches and corrosion. Its Oyster bracelet with Oysterlock safety clasp offers a secure and comfortable fit, perfect for both the racetrack and everyday wear. Powered by the Rolex Caliber 4130 self-winding mechanical movement, the Cosmograph Daytona delivers precise and reliable timekeeping, with a chronograph function accurate to within 1/8th of a second. Its innovative Parachrom hairspring and Paraflex shock absorbers enhance its performance, ensuring optimal functionality in any racing condition. The iconic dial of the Cosmograph Daytona features contrasting sub-dials in black, providing a striking and legible display of elapsed time. With its screw-down pushers and Triplock winding crown, this watch offers waterproofness to a depth of 100 meters (330 feet), making it suitable for water sports as well.
