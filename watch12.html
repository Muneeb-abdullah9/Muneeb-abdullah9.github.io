Rolex Yacht-Master 42

Price: $15,000

about this item:

Introducing the Rolex Yacht-Master 42, a symbol of elegance and adventure on the high seas. Crafted with meticulous attention to detail, this timepiece embodies the spirit of maritime exploration while exuding luxury and sophistication. At its core lies the Rolex Calibre 3235, a self-winding mechanical movement renowned for its precision and reliability. With innovative features like the Chronergy escapement and Paraflex shock absorbers, it ensures impeccable timekeeping performance, even in the most challenging environments. Boasting a power reserve of approximately 70 hours, the Yacht-Master 42 is ready to accompany you on extended journeys with unwavering accuracy. The design of the Yacht-Master 42 is both timeless and functional. Its 42mm case, crafted from 18ct white gold, commands attention with its refined elegance. The platinum bidirectional rotatable bezel, featuring a matte black Cerachrom insert, not only adds a touch of sophistication but also serves a practical purpose, allowing for precise timekeeping during sailing adventures. With luminescent hour markers and hands on the black dial, readability is assured, even in low-light conditions. Waterproof to a depth of 100 meters (330 feet) and equipped with an Oyster case, the Yacht-Master 42 is built to withstand the rigors of maritime exploration. Whether navigating the open ocean or attending a formal event ashore, the Rolex Yacht-Master 42 is the ultimate companion for those who seek both style and performance in equal measure.
