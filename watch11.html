about this item:

The Rolex Datejust 36 stands as a beacon of timeless elegance and horological mastery, embodying the perfect blend of sophistication and functionality. Renowned for its iconic design and enduring appeal, the Datejust 36 remains a cornerstone of Rolex's esteemed collection, cherished by watch enthusiasts and discerning individuals alike. At the heart of the Datejust 36 beats the Rolex Caliber 3235, a self-winding mechanical movement celebrated for its precision and reliability. Equipped with Rolex's patented Chronergy escapement and Paraflex shock absorbers, the Caliber 3235 ensures impeccable timekeeping performance in any situation. With a power reserve of approximately 70 hours, the Datejust 36 keeps ticking long after lesser timepieces have succumbed to the passage of time. Featuring a classic and timeless design, the Datejust 36 exudes understated luxury and sophistication. Available in a variety of materials, including 18k gold, Everose gold, and stainless steel, it offers versatility to suit a range of tastes and preferences. The iconic fluted bezel, magnified date window at 3 o'clock, and Jubilee bracelet further enhance its allure, while the Oyster case provides water resistance up to 100 meters (330 feet), ensuring durability and reliability in any environment. Whether worn as a statement of refinement in the boardroom or as a companion for special occasions, the Rolex Datejust 36 is the epitome of timeless style and elegance. With its impeccable craftsmanship, enduring design, and legendary reliability, the Datejust 36 continues to captivate watch enthusiasts and remain a cherished symbol of achievement and success.
