about this item:

Introducing the Rolex Air-King, a timepiece inspired by the golden age of aviation and crafted with precision, reliability, and timeless elegance. Originally created to honor the pilots of the British Royal Air Force during World War II, the Air-King has evolved into an iconic symbol of aviation heritage and horological excellence. At the heart of the Air-King beats the Rolex Caliber 3131 movement, a self-winding mechanical marvel renowned for its accuracy and durability. Equipped with a Parachrom hairspring and Paraflex shock absorbers, the Caliber 3131 ensures optimal performance even in the most demanding conditions. With a power reserve of approximately 48 hours, the Air-King is ready to accompany pilots on their high-altitude adventures. Featuring a clean, straightforward design and bold numerals, the Air-King pays tribute to the pioneering spirit of aviation. Its distinctive black dial, luminous hour markers, and signature green Rolex logo create a striking contrast that enhances readability in all lighting conditions. The Oyster case, crafted from corrosion-resistant Oystersteel, ensures water resistance up to 100 meters (330 feet), making the Air-King a versatile timepiece suitable for everyday wear. Whether soaring through the skies or navigating the urban jungle, the Rolex Air-King is the ultimate companion for those who dare to dream and explore. With its timeless design, exceptional craftsmanship, and legendary reliability, the Air-King embodies the spirit of adventure and innovation, inspiring wearers to reach new heights in style and sophistication.
