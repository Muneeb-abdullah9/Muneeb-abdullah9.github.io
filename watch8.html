Rolex Deepsea

Price: $9,000

about this item:

Introducing the Rolex Deepsea, an extraordinary timepiece engineered to push the boundaries of exploration in the deepest reaches of the ocean. Born from Rolex's relentless pursuit of innovation and performance, the Deepsea is the ultimate tool watch for professional divers and adventurers alike. With its unparalleled depth rating, rugged construction, and cutting-edge technology, the Deepsea stands as a symbol of Rolex's commitment to excellence in extreme conditions. At the heart of the Deepsea lies the Rolex Caliber 3235 movement, a self-winding mechanical marvel renowned for its accuracy and reliability. Equipped with a Parachrom hairspring and Paraflex shock absorbers, the Caliber 3235 ensures optimal performance even in the most challenging environments. With a power reserve of approximately 70 hours, the Deepsea is ready to accompany divers on extended underwater expeditions. Featuring a helium escape valve and a unidirectional rotatable Cerachrom bezel, the Deepsea is designed to withstand the immense pressure of deep-sea exploration. Water-resistant to an incredible depth of 3,900 meters (12,800 feet), it provides divers with the confidence to explore the ocean's depths with unwavering reliability. Crafted from corrosion-resistant Oystersteel and equipped with a scratch-resistant sapphire crystal, the Deepsea combines ruggedness with elegance, making it the ultimate companion for underwater adventures. Whether diving into the abyss or conquering new frontiers, the Rolex Deepsea is the pinnacle of performance, precision, and durability, embodying the spirit of exploration in its purest form.
